Our Story

A practice built on
trust.

Dr. Séamus O'Sullivan opened on Main Street in 1987 with one belief: dental care should feel like visiting a trusted friend. He built relationships with families across North Cork that lasted decades.

In 2008, daughter Dr. Sinéad O'Sullivan joined after her degree at UCC and a Fellowship in Dublin. She expanded the practice into the purpose-built surgery it is today.

Sinéad's son Ciarán is now training at Trinity — the third generation. Dr. Séamus still comes in on Thursdays. If you've been a patient long enough, you'll have seen all three.
